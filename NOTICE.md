# NOTICE

FaceChan is free software, released under the MIT licence. You can use it, modify it, run it, and build on it without restriction.

There are no conditions. There is one request.

---

## Attribution

If you build something with FaceChan — a fork, a hosted instance, a derivative project — a credit saying:

> **Based on FaceChan by Kino**

somewhere reasonable (your README, your about page, your footer) would mean a great deal. It doesn't have to be prominent. It doesn't have to be loud. Just a nod to where it came from.

This is not a legal requirement. It's a human one.

---

## Donations

If FaceChan has been useful to you and you'd like to say so with something more than words, donation details are in [DONATE.md](DONATE.md).

No account. No subscription. No KYC. Completely optional and genuinely appreciated.

---

## Why this file exists

The licence doesn't require any of this. Most people in open source know that attribution and donations live on trust and community norms, not legal clauses.

Kino is one person. This took a long time to build. If it helps you build something — that's the whole point. A mention costs nothing and it keeps the thread of where things came from visible in the world.

That's all.
